Rebellion #5
The (Almost) Spring Edition

February 20th . 6.30pm-Late . The Alb

We want to start the new year in style and are packing a lot into our first Rebellion of 2014.

We've invited some of the digital industries leading talent from both Shropshire and further afield covering a range of topics and disciplines from front end coding and design to business and marketing.

There will be three headline speakers, two five minute lightning talks and five minutes at the end of the evening to give you opportunity to share any news or announcements you have. What more could you want?

Tickets are only £10 per person, doors open at 6.30pm with the first speaker starting at 7pm.

The Speakers

Syd Lawrence

  • Link
  • LinkedIn
  • Twitter

Syd Lawrence is cofounder of We Make Awesome Sh, and is a developer evangelist at Twilio. He's a developer who is interested in too much tech to be particularly good at any of them.

He makes stuff for fun, for profit, for lols. He's made things for companies such as Coca Cola and Microsoft, and musicians such as The Rolling Stones, Swedish House Mafia, and Kylie Minogue.

Dave Coveney

  • Link
  • LinkedIn
  • Twitter

David feels that these conference bios are a bit like online dating profiles. There’s a desire to seem interesting, not scary or too weird, and to suggest that this is somebody who you can tolerate for at least half an hour without you having to excuse yourself to the bathroom. So hey. How you doing?

Oh, and he accidentally wrote a useful but dangerous little search/replace script that became crazy popular even though it was one of the smallest bits of work in his career!

Jon Lewis

  • Link
  • LinkedIn
  • Twitter

In 2013, Jon was asked to build up the marketing side of Zengenti and has spent his time since then rebranding the whole business. As a result of his work he’s been shortlisted for the CIM New Marketer of the Year award.

Whether at home or work, he talks about the same things throughout the day - often he’s simply trying to look clever by reciting the latest Audible book he’s working through.
