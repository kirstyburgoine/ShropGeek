February 20th . 6.30pm-Late . The Alb

We want to start the new year in style and are packing a lot into our first Rebellion of 2014.

We've invited some of the digital industries leading talent from both Shropshire and further afield covering a range of topics and disciplines from front end coding and design to business and marketing.

There will be three headline speakers, two five minute lightning talks and five minutes at the end of the evening to give you opportunity to share any news or announcements you have. What more could you want?

Tickets are only £10 per person, doors open at 6.30pm with the first speaker starting at 7pm.
